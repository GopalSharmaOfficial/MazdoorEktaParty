Why Your Support Matters

The Mazdoor Ekta Party relies on donations from supporters like you to continue our important work. Your contributions help us:

  • Organize grassroots campaigns for workers' rights
  • Provide legal aid to workers facing exploitation
  • Run educational programs on labor laws and rights
  • Advocate for policy changes at local and national levels
  • Conduct research on labor issues and economic inequality

Unlike corporate-backed parties, we don't have wealthy donors funding our work. We depend on small contributions from ordinary citizens who believe in our cause.

Donations to Political Parties are eligible for 100% Income Tax exemption under Section 80GGB (for Companies) and Section 80GGC (for Others) as per the Income-tax Act, 1961.
