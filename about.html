About Mazdoor Ekta Party

"From the upliftment of the last person to the welfare of all"

Our Story

Founded in 2019, Mazdoor Ekta Party was born out of the urgent need to address the growing inequality in our society and to give voice to the working class of our nation.

The party was established by a coalition of labor union leaders, social activists, and progressive thinkers who shared a common vision: to create a society where every worker, farmer, and citizen has equal opportunities and rights.

Since our inception, we have been dedicated to fighting for economic justice, social equality, and the rights of the working class across the nation.

Protest: MEP vs Delhi Government

Mazdoor Ekta Party organized a powerful protest against the AAP government led by Arvind Kejriwal in Delhi.

We raised our voice against the policies that failed to address the concerns of workers, laborers, and common citizens of Delhi.

Our party stands firm in holding the government accountable and fighting for the rights of the working class.

Mazdoor Ekta Party in News

Mazdoor Ekta Party's electoral journey has been covered by leading news channels across India.

From our debut in Delhi Assembly Elections 2020 to MCD Elections and Bihar Assembly Elections 2025, media has recognized our growing presence in Indian politics.

These news coverages highlight our commitment to democratic participation and the trust people place in our vision for workers' rights.

Our Achievements

2019

Mazdoor Ekta Party was officially formed with representation from 15 states.

2020

Contested NCT Of Delhi Legislative Assembly Election 2020 on 3 seats.

2021

Successfully advocated for minimum wage increases in three states and expanded party membership to over 100,000.

2025 | Bihar Election

Contested Bihar Legislative Assembly Election 2025 with candidate SATYA NARAYAN PASWAN from 78-Kusheshwar Asthan(SC) constituency.
