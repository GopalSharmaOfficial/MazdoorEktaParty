About Mazdoor Ekta Party

"From the upliftment of the last person to upliftment of all"

Our Story

Founded in 2019, Mazdoor Ekta Party was born out of the urgent need to address the growing inequality in our society and to give voice to the working class of our nation.

The party was established by a coalition of labor union leaders, social activists, and progressive thinkers who shared a common vision: to create a society where every worker, farmer, and citizen has equal opportunities and rights.

Since our inception, we have been dedicated to fighting for economic justice, social equality, and the rights of the working class across the nation.

Our Vision

We envision a society where:

  • Every worker receives fair wages and dignified working conditions
  • Economic policies benefit the many, not just the few
  • Education and healthcare are accessible to all citizens
  • Social justice and equality are fundamental principles
  • Environmental sustainability guides our development

Core Values

Equality

We believe in equal rights, opportunities, and treatment for all citizens regardless of their background, caste, religion, or gender.

Solidarity

We stand united with workers, farmers, and marginalized communities in their struggles for justice and dignity.

Democracy

We are committed to democratic principles both within our party and in our vision for governance.

Economic Justice

We fight for fair distribution of resources and opportunities to reduce inequality.

Sustainability

We advocate for environmentally sustainable policies that protect our planet for future generations.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty, transparency, and accountability in all our actions.

Our Achievements

2019

Mazdoor Ekta Party was officially formed with representation from 15 states.

2020

Led nationwide protests for workers' rights during the pandemic and provided relief to thousands of affected families.

2021

Successfully advocated for minimum wage increases in three states and expanded party membership to over 100,000.
