Welcome to Mazdoor Ekta Party

Founded in 2019, the Mazdoor Ekta Party is committed to creating a society where every worker, farmer, and citizen has equal opportunities and rights.

Our mission is to fight for economic justice, social equality, and the rights of the working class across the nation. We believe in the power of collective action and solidarity to bring about meaningful change in society.
